WILLIAMS GLOBAL (Formally known as Will Event Management) was established in September 1998 with no less than  15 years experience from over 1,000 different functions and events. Today, with sheer dedication and explosive creativity, we have developed into a mature, stable and reliable corporate source of pure event management genius.

Today, Williams Global is powered by over 10 main operators by over 30 main operation staffs. Their expertise covers event arrangements, exhibitions, product launching and brand building. With such a huge group of enthusiastic crew, we are sure to be able to handle your requirements. Simply ask! Challenge is just another part of our daily operations. 

Our edge is our personal approach. Hands on, being there and almost everywhere! We always listen, open to great ideas, and keep your goals in mind. Just let us know what that is, then sit back and let us bedazzle your crowd,because there is magic in what we do here. All you need to do is believe! 

It is not difficult to understand why we are able to provide so much to our customers. It is simply because of our hands on attitude. We are always the first in contact to everything: progress, problems and unexpected turn of events. We are almost always within the crowd – your target customers to know what they need most or want best. That is how we can give you the best solutions to your requirements, give you value-added ideas and guide. We strive to provide your entertainment resources and entire marketing needs. Turn to us for a new idea to capture a new market segment, intend to pull crowds, discuss the color of  your banners, etc. 
We concern about your needs and end in mind, will customize to your precise requirements, up to every inch!
